Description

Lasagna: Layers of wide pasta, seasoned ground meat, creamy ricotta cheese, tangy tomato sauce, and aromatic herbs like garlic and basil. Baked until golden and bubbling, it's a comforting Italian classic, beloved for its rich flavors and versatility. A hearty indulgence that satisfies with every bite.

  • Onion and garlic

Steps:

  4. Bake
  5. Serve
